Website Critiques

Questions

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Website Critiques-Utah National Parks.

  1. My first impression is that the website was that it looks nice, with nice colors and a pretty attractive picture.
  2. The site has a nice nagivation bar at the top of the website with links to different sections.
  3. I think the site structure makes sense because it is easy to use and the sections at the top are useful to help find the information needed.
  4. I think it does enhance the website because it provides beautiful visuals.
  5. I believe it took less than 5 seconds to load which is really fast.
  6. Yes, the text color and size were readable. Yes an image was used in the background and it did enhance from the website.
  7. Yes, the site has a navigation bar and it was very well done.
  8. The site has a nice color scheme and made it easy to looks around.
  9. It responded very well to smaller window sizes.
  10. The site offers very details itinaries to visit multiple national parks, well organize which could help travelers.
